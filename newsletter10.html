IITians ruling the Indian Unicorn Club 2021

2021 has seen an enormous rise in Indian startups turning into Unicorns. From 2021 until 17 of September, 28 Indian Startups have turned into unicorns with a value exceeding $1 Billion. This brings the number of Unicorns in India to 65, holding its position at number 3 in the world after 122 Unicorns in the USA and 91 in China. 

According to the statistics, more than half of the Unicorn founders this year are from IITs. Most of them belong to the 4 oldest IITs, including IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, IIT Kharagpur, IIT Kanpur, and BIT Pilani, playing an important role. 20 out of 28 Startups listed in the Unicorns 2021 have at least one Co-founder from the above five institutes.

Further, Listing the companies, we have Mobile Premier League, Network, Grofers, upGrad, BharatPe, OfBusiness, BlackBuck, Gupshup, ShareChat, Meesho, Innovaccer, Infra.Market and many more famous companies by IITians had the guts to bring their dreams into reality and turn their startup into a billion-dollar company. This year IITians seem to be ruling the startup ecosystem, with every second unicorn founder turning out to be from an IIT. 

These numbers turn out to result from great history and outstanding Alum Base at these oldest IITs that have helped the students convince the investors to trust them. IITs build a massive network of great minds by providing the best technical education and a holistic atmosphere for students to learn from their seniors and co-working batch-mates.

Omnichannel eyewear retailer Unicorn, Lenskartnow aims to create micro-entrepreneurs through its Home-Try-On (HTO) business. Over the last two years, the HTO business had witnessed 200 percent growth with 300 super-star executives, offering over eight lakh eyeglasses and conducting 10 lakh eye tests across ten cities every month. The team now aims to create over 2,000 microentrepreneurs in India's Tier-II towns by 2022.

The company is now planning to further expand the HTO business model by incorporating micro-entrepreneurs. Peeyush Bansal, Co-founder, and CEO, Lenskart, said HTO is a robust customer service-based model based on Lenskart's DNA of customer obsession.

India and Singapore will link their respective fast payment systems -- Unified Payments Interface (UPI) and PayNow -- to enable users to make instant, low-cost fund transfers on a reciprocal basis. 

PayNow is the fast payment system of Singapore that enables peer-to-peer funds transfer service, available to retail customers through participating banks and Non-Bank Financial Institutions (NFIs) in Singapore.

RBI said the UPI-PayNow linkage is a significant milestone in developing infrastructure for cross-border payments between India and Singapore and closely aligns with the G20's financial inclusion priorities of driving faster, cheaper, and more transparent cross-border payments.

Khatabook, started as Kyte.ai in 2016, has now become a crucial factor of MSME digitalization. Inspired by the manual Khata diaries in India, It offers digital tools that help enterprises and small businesses empower themselves. With the slogan of "Digital India ka Digital Khata, Khatabook has digitized bookkeeping with services like Pagarkhata, Cashbook,  Biz Analyst, and its conventional finance management. Khatabook saw a hockey stick growth curve since its launch, now accommodating 10 million active users monthly that sum up to a total of 264 million users on the platform with exceptional word-of-mouth publicity.

Apna is a platform that provides communities for skilled professionals and is a job marketplace for blue-collar workers. Upskilling the rising workforce, Apna aims to disrupt the employment gap and hiring challenges with over 1.5 Lakh companies and startups enrolled. It is the fastest startup to enter the unicorn club this year, multiplying at a rate of 125x in the past 15 months. Apna has reached 28 Cities in India and aims to reach 300 cities and 4,000 towns with 5 Million jobs available and more than 70 job categories and vertical communities. It is the peer-to-peer network and publicity among the Apna users, that is helping the company to grow more.
