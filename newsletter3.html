STARTUPS UP AGAINST THE COVID PANDEMIC

With the ongoing pandemic in its full swing, every individual is bound to put in his/her effort to the maximum extent possible. Resources, energies, and mindshare of leaders, entrepreneurs have to be synergized to minimize the damage of the pandemic.

Along the same lines, the NIDHI4COVID 2.0 initiative has been specially devised as a rapid response to support startup-driven solutions, which would help tackle the challenging situations that we may face in the coming future, similar to what we saw in the second wave. NIDHI4COVID 2.0 is a special initiative of the Government of India (GoI), to support indigenous solutions and innovative products that will better equip our country to combat the COVID-19 pandemic. In the current situation, startups can play a critical role in bringing new technology and products to market, thereby strengthening our country on multiple fronts in the ongoing COVID fight. Some startups have basic resources but they need mentoring and funding to establish themselves on a major scale. As a result, DST's new effort aims to identify and support deserving startups with much-needed assistance in scaling up their technologies, assisting them in reaching the product deployment stage as quickly as possible.

Kgp Startups have also come up to the need of the hour to facilitate society in these challenging times. Alok Munshi and Tanishq Bansal, fellow Kgpians, established one such entrepreneurial project called " KioGo." They provide Ambulance and Taxi services covering huge demography over Madhya Pradesh at a nominal cost via their app.

Without a doubt, NIDHI4COVID 2.0 will encourage blooming startups to groom, given the numerous incentives they provide. In turn, this would ameliorate the state of the nation as well as the startup ecosystem of the country. Click here to read further.

Mother Your Child

"We take care of a child's Holistic Health" goes the tagline of Mother Your Child, a healthcare service providing urgent virtual pediatrics care for a child's emotional and physical well-being.

The MYC Team consists of Piyush Sanduja, an IIT KGP alum with ten years of experience working in various business sectors. Dr. Monica Singh is a highly qualified doctor with ten years of medical expertise and Sai Gopichand, a graduate from IIT Kanpur, handles technical aspects. 

MYC adopts AI/ML methods for predictions and focuses on screening tools for early detection of illnesses and efficiently addressing people's concerns. It solves the issue of inadequate and inefficient healthcare for children. They plan to open clinics and eliminate unnecessary trips to the hospitals when not necessary by giving solutions to distressed parents through their mobile app.
