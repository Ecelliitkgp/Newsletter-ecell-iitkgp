NFT, Their recent rise and popularity

Just when we thought things couldn't get any weirder, an artist sold an Invisible NFT for $18000 a few months ago. So what exactly is an NFT. A non-fungible token is a data unit recorded on a digital ledger known as a blockchain that certifies a digital asset as unique and thus non-transferable. It is non-fungible; instead, it is a one-of-a-kind asset since an NFT has data that distinguishes it from every other NFT. It is used to represent digital data like photographs, audio, video, and different data files. According to the study, The NFTs raised a total of US$305 million between February 2020 and April 2021. Nifty Gateway, a non-fungible token online auction platform for digital art, launched 3,009 NFTs with over 145,600 editions created for purchase during this time, representing digital artworks by 272 artists. Before the recent surge in the use of NFTs, creators faced revenue limitations from their digital creative works because copies could be easily made infinite times and distributed throughout the internet without degradation of quality, making it difficult for creators to monetize their digital creative works in the past. Speaking of monetizing creative work, a woman sold 'her love' as an NFT. What other crazy NFT stories have you heard?

Enerjazz

Enerjazz is the startup founded in 2018 by IIT Kharagpur alumni Ravi Bharihoke and Pratik Ranjan Das. It is a startup that is building a battery swapping network for 2M+electric vehicles in India. Ivy Homes

Ivy Homes is a Bangalore-based startup founded by IIT Kharagpur alumni Abhilash Narahari and IIT Kanpur alumni Pritam Thakur. It uses a proprietary algorithm to estimate property prices and offer instant liquidity to homeowners. The algorithm uses 30+ parameters in evaluating the property. Currently, it charges zero fees in this process. The startup was founded after realizing that the process of buying and selling homes was completely broken and inefficient. The efficiency of the startup helps the transaction of the property complete in just 2-3 weeks. Read More.
