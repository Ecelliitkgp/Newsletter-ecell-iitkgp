Indian retail market has a new player

The Indian retail business has a new market player named ‘7Eleven’. On 7th of October 2021, Reliance Industries officially announced that Reliance retail has entered into a master franchise agreement with the 7-Eleven company for launch of 7-Eleven convenience stores in India and this deal has taken the market by such a storm that the stocks of Reliance rose by 1.5%.

Started as Southland Ice Company in 1974, as a small retail shop chain in the USA. Things changed for the company when they opened their stores in Japan. In the next 30 years the company became so successful that it had a total of 11,310 stores and had a total sales of $22 billion.

The company has achieved this success due to its ability of successful  micromanagement in the supply chain. It manages the supply and demand of the store at local level analysing the behaviour of their customers and has a strong network among the supply chain from the 1970s. It also holds the expertise to serve local food and snacks making it like a mini-restaurant. Now they have everything managed by Artificial Intelligence with more than 50 years of experience which is a great gift for Reliance. Reliance with 12,000 stores with Reliance Smart, Reliance Fresh, Jio Mart, Reliance Digital; and now with 7-Eleven can make Reliance a powerful retail ecosystem. These things can make things difficult for emerging startups like BigBasket and Groffers and especially Amazon who is facing a cutting edge competition from Reliance.

Things are still not going to be so easy for 7-Eleven as there are already a lot of big players in the game like D-Mart and Big Bazaar. The company has seen a major backfall in Indonesia where their experience failed in understanding the Indonesian market and facing the big players of the country like Alfarmart and Indomaret.

FLIPSHOPE

While studying at IIT Kharagpur in 2014, the Founders, Ashutosh Goyal and Gaurav Kumar, conceived the idea for 'Flipshope,' and they worked with zeal and created the Flipshope Google Chrome Extension on October 1, 2014.

Flipshope is a website that assists consumers in saving money while purchasing online. It has a team that scours the retail industry for the greatest deals and offers and exposes them to clients looking for discounts on products that would otherwise be prohibitively expensive. Flipshope is a rapidly expanding company with plans to expand into other markets. They're quickly becoming a household name in India; the most downloaded Chrome extension in the category of online shopping with over 2 million downloads, over 20 million impressions worldwide, and 150,000 daily active users.
