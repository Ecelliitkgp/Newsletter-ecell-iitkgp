The Rise of India’s Cloud Kitchen Industry

As a result of the COVID-19 pandemic-related limitations, India's slow cooking cloud kitchen sector has exploded. Cloud kitchens are restaurants that solely accept online orders for delivery and do not have a physical location. To increase brand awareness, they frequently engage with food aggregation delivery firms like Swiggy and Zomato. According to a report by RedSeer Management Consulting, cloud kitchens are set to be a $2 billion industry in India by 2024, up from $400 million in 2019. 

The closure of restaurants due to the lockdown has pushed a majority of the population to opt for either home-cooked meals or depend on food brought in through deliveries giving boom to this Cloud Industry. Given that this market is currently growing at such a fast pace, businesses like FreshMenu and Rebel Foods are likely to emerge in the coming months. According to a study an estimated 18 percent single-unit restaurants won't be able to re-start their units, leaving delivery the only way to keep the brand alive for existing players. Given how things are unfolding, Cloud kitchening is unquestionably the way of the future. Read More

Innovaccer Inc., a Silicon Valley-based healthcare company founded by IIT, Kharagpur graduates Kanav Hasija and Abhinav Shashank has been profiled in LinkedIn’s 5th Annual Top Startups 2021 list. The list reveals 50 young, emerging companies chosen for their high employee growth, jobseeker interest and member engagement with the company. In order to make it on the list, startups must be less than  7 years old, with at least 50 employees, be privately held and headquartered in the U.S. Some of the many reasons Innovaccer made the list include the immense value its team gains through healthcare industry leaders in the company's leadership, strategic advisory board, and customer base, all of whom work closely with and mentor its employees which include nationally known physician and business leaders, academics, technology experts, entrepreneurs and partners. Innovaccer's team has scaled its customer base and revenue tremendously in the past five years, achieving more than 100% Year over Year growth.
