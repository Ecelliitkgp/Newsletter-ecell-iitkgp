How tech can make Logistics more viable

Logistics is one of the most critical parts of a Company. It can affect profitability and brand value to a huge extent. Companies must constantly adopt the use of technology to sustain logistics in the long run. To adapt to changing climate and environmental conditions, the logistics sector is harnessing innovative technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI) and building strong transportation chains with the help of various Computer Algorithms. 

The Logistics sector collects a lot of data regarding the operations, like tracking different transport modes collected through telematics. However, such information was not fully utilized. AI is now routinely employed for predictive analytics in intelligent transportation, route planning, and demand planning. To scale inventory management, warehouse operations are increasingly being combined with augmented guidance and robotic systems. The AI-powered insights are pretty insightful in cases like temperature-controlled trucks delivering perishable items. 

Technology has the power to make the sector much more flexible. It would benefit everyone from the consumer to the company. It also has environmental benefits.

Flippy

Flippy, a FinTech startup founded by Srinidhi Moodalagiri, an Alumni of IIT Kharagpur and Srivar Harlalka, aims at making crypto investing more fun and less confusing. Crypto investment is taken with precaution due to the risk and the wide variety of crypto coins available coupled with very little knowledge. While people want to find the next Bitcoin, they are afraid not to end with a fraud coin. Flippy takes on all these problems and comes with the concept of the basket. Each basket contains cryptos curated by experts for safe investing. The investor doesn't have to worry about researching each crypto, and they can pick a basket based on the theme and be assured of the background research. Check out Flippy
